1600M SEASON RECORD

April 9, 2022

Run Notes

Chilly weather at the Don Boyer Invitational. I took it easy the first lap, which led to feeling comfortable and relaxed throughout. I stuck onto the fast pack in the heat for the first two laps, but we spread out and I ran alone for the second two. Came second in my heat. Strong race for a rough season.
