THE 24 IN 24

June 11, 2022

Run Notes

The Wilde Lake track team decided to run one mile at the top of each hour for 24 hours. We started off with a full 10 people and ended with only 4. Ran miles at home, the pool, the track, and more. While mental fatigue was tough, especially in the wee hours, muscle cramping was tougher yet. Pretty sure I injured my knee on those last few miles.

Run Stats

Distance: 24 miles

Moving Time: 3:55:32

Avg Pace: 9:49 min/mi
